Cau 9: 
Dung luong 1 ranh = 20000
Toc do quay = 3200(vong/phut)  
Do dai ban ghi = 1000
He so khoi = 8 

Toc do doc du lieu trong 1 phut = 20000 * 3200 = 64000000 (bytes)
Dung luong 1 khoi = 8 * 1000 = 8000 (bytes)
-> thoi gian doc 1 khoi = 8000/64000000 = 8/64000 (phut) = 8*60000 / 64000 (ms) = 30/4 (ms) = 7.5 ms
-> Dap an E

Trung binh tim kiem trung binh = thoi gian tim kiem trong ranh TB + thoi gian dinh vi dau tu TB
